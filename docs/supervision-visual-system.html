SVN.02 · FOUNDATIONS · TYPOGRAPHY

Typography.

Inter for UI. JetBrains Mono for any number that represents a measurement — IPs, FPS, timestamps, retention, uptime. The mono treatment does the most work for the precision aesthetic.

Sans scale (Inter)

display 40 / 48 · 600 Supervision
title 28 / 36 · 600 Health Monitoring
heading-1 22 / 28 · 600 Recently triggered events
heading-2 18 / 24 · 600 Connected servers
heading-3 16 / 22 · 600 Camera 03 — Front entrance
body-lg 15 / 22 · 400 Add the cameras at this site. Supervision will discover Hikvision and Dahua devices automatically; switch to Pro mode to enter connection strings directly.
body 14 / 20 · 400 Default UI text size. Used for table cells, form fields, navigation, and most labels in the application.
body-sm 13 / 18 · 400 Secondary information, helper text under inputs, smaller table cells, supplementary descriptions.
caption 12 / 16 · 500 Camera offline · 2 minutes ago
micro 11 / 14 · 500 Reserved · uppercase tracking

Mono scale (JetBrains Mono)

Used for measurements, identifiers, and operational facts. Tabular figures are enabled so columns of numbers align.

mono-lg 20 / 28 · 500 2,847
mono 14 / 20 · 400 192.168.1.201:554
mono-sm 13 / 18 · 400 /Streaming/Channels/101
mono-cap 11 / 14 · 500 23:36:14 · 30 FPS · 4.2 Mbps
Decision rule: a number representing a measurement, identifier, or operational fact uses mono. A number in prose ("you have 3 cameras offline") uses Inter.
